Hi, I’m Robert. I’m a web developer and data analyst based in New York focused on making complex ideas tangible and easily understood. My favorite languages for this job are Julia for ad-hoc analysis and visualization, d3 and Vega(-Lite) for building browser-based visualizations, and Rust for building long-term data pipelines (thanks to the fantastic Polars library). Outside of work I enjoy writing more Rust, reading, and taking photographs.

Quarantine Name That Tune Trivia

 rben01/quarantine-music-trivia
A game I put together to give friends something to play while hanging out virtually during the coronavirus pandemic. It’s like trivia, but the questions are all audio clips from well-known songs with prompts such as “Name the song”, “Name the album”, and “What movie had this song as the theme?”.

The site does not discard videos that have been scrolled out of view, so it will use a lot of memory. Refreshing should purge memory, but I recommend only playing on desktop for the time being. Hopefully I’ll get around to fixing this at some point.
